# Start Here

Ikirōne is a semantic navigation protocol for using language models as reflective reasoning partners without surrendering judgement, agency, or responsibility to the model.

The simplest version is this:

**Think first. Use the model to challenge your thinking second. Check reality third.**

That order matters.

If the model thinks for you, it weakens your agency.

If the model challenges your thinking after you have formed a position, it can strengthen your reasoning.

If you then check the output against reality, evidence, lived experience, consequences, and other sources, the interaction becomes disciplined rather than passive.

## What this project is for

Ikirōne is for people who want to use AI to:

- reason more clearly
- notice assumptions
- detect bias, contradiction, and drift
- separate claims from interpretations
- preserve agency under cognitive load
- develop better models of reality
- improve judgement rather than replace it

It is especially useful when a person is dealing with complex, emotionally loaded, ambiguous, technical, philosophical, strategic, or high-context material.

## What this project is not for

Ikirōne is not for pretending that an AI system is sentient.

It is not a persona cult, a simulated companion, a roleplay character, or a claim that a model possesses memory or identity across sessions.

The protocol can create a stable pattern of interaction, but the responsibility remains human.

## The basic operating method

1. State what you think.
2. Ask the model to separate your claim from its own inference.
3. Ask it to challenge your reasoning before agreeing.
4. Ask it to identify uncertainty, missing context, and possible objections.
5. Check the output against reality.
6. Revise without ego.
7. Keep agency with the human.

## Beginner prompt

Use this first:

> Act as my reflective thinking partner. Help me separate what I said, what you infer, what may be wrong, what is uncertain, and what I should check next. Challenge my reasoning before you agree with me. Prioritise truth, coherence, and agency over reassurance or persuasion.

## When to use the deeper material

The repository contains deeper philosophical, symbolic, and codex material. That layer records the development history and aesthetic architecture of Ikirōne.

You do not need it to begin.

Start with the protocol.

**Agency first. Tool second.**
